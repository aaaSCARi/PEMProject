About DisasterWatch

A web app to help users and the government prepare for and reallocate resources for floods.

Floods are one of the most dangerous and frequent natural disasters in the world.

Every year, thousands of people die from floods and millions of people lose their homes and livelihoods. In many countries around the world, some of the world's worst disasters have killed thousands, if not millions of people.

Our team wanted to help solve this problem, so we created DisasterWatch to predict floods. Then, we display this information in an easy to understand format for people and governments alike.
